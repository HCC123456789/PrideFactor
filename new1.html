Pride Factor Launches Online Academy to Inspire and Guide South African Youth

Pride Factor, the life-skills academy founded in 2014 to ‘Inspire Young Minds’, is proud to announce the launch of its Online Academy to guide teenagers and young adults on how to achieve successful careers and fulfilling lifestyles.

Using the feedback received from thousands of surveys completed at approximately 90 live events where more than 45 000 people have been inspired to improve their quality of life, Pride Factor has developed a modern approach to education that provides a state-of-the-art, rewards-based E-learning platform aimed primarily at improving the quality of life for young people all over Africa.

Consisting of online life-skills, work-readiness and entrepreneurship courses designed to equip young people with the tools and guidelines required to find jobs or start their own businesses, each episode consists of a short explanatory video clip presented by experts in their field along with a thought-provoking task and links to supporting information.

Current courses include:

  • • Basic life skills for Teenagers (13 episodes)
  • • Advanced work skills for Young Adults (13 episodes)
  • • Entrepreneurship 101 (5 Episodes)
  • • Financial Literacy 101 (Under construction)

Academy Courses Sponsored for Over 220 Students

Pride Factor is delighted to announce that more than 220 students already have access to the Academy courses thanks to generous sponsorship from companies, NGO’s and individuals who are passionate about addressing the problems of unemployment, poverty and inequality in Africa.

The bulk of the funding to date has been donated by Learn To Trade and the Greg Secker Foundation whose founder and CEO aims to provide young people, regardless of their background, with practical life strategies and emotional development tools.

Additional funds were kindly contributed by Juan Botha (Events Chameleon), Anna Shilina (Empanna Properties), Babsy Shilubane (Daughters of Destiny), Linda Liquorish (The Rock), Edward Martin and Dr Norman Cahi (Cahi Dental Practitioners).

Become a Pride Factor Academy Student or Sponsor

Teenagers and young adults who are enthusiastic about improving their chances of securing paying jobs or starting their own businesses are invited to register for Pride Factor Academy courses by visiting http://pridefactor.co.za/registration/ and completing the online registration form. You will be given access to the course material on a first-come, first-served basis once sponsorship funds have been secured.

An individual payment gateway that enables students to sign up and pay for courses is currently under construction.

Companies, organisations and individuals can find out more about giving needy students access to the courses by contacting hello@pridefactor.co.za. Sponsors can choose to supply the details of students they wish to support or sponsor students who have already registered for courses.
